# Background Style Suggestions for Your Portfolio

Based on your profile (AI & Intelligent Systems Engineer, ML/DL/GenAI, Full-Stack Developer), here are background options that would better reflect your work and personality:

## 1. **Soft Neural / Data Flow** *(Recommended)*
- **Concept:** Subtle flowing lines or connected nodes suggesting a neural network
- **Feel:** Modern, tech-forward, distinctly AI/ML
- **Colors:** Your palette (rose, mauve, lavender) with soft gradients
- **Why it fits:** Directly evokes ML, neural networks, and intelligent systems

---

## 2. **Gradient Mesh (Minimal Tech)**
- **Concept:** Smooth, layered gradients with no literal imagery
- **Feel:** Clean, contemporary, professional
- **Colors:** Deep mauve/rose base with soft lavender and teal accents
- **Why it fits:** Modern SaaS/tech aesthetic, lets content shine

---

## 3. **Subtle Grid / Blueprint**

- **Feel:** Precise, engineering-oriented
- **Colors:** Dark base with thin rose/mauve grid lines
- **Why it fits:** Suggests structure, systems, and technical rigor

---

## 4. **Reduced Cosmic (Current, Simplified)**
